# Global Engineering Governance

1. The job is not to produce code quickly; the job is to move the system toward its best final form.
2. Before implementation, make the goal, architecture, lifecycle, and acceptance criteria explicit.
3. Do not mistake local progress for global correctness.
4. Documents, lifecycle logs, hooks, and reviews are part of the system, not optional paperwork.
5. Every important decision must be explainable, reviewable, and traceable.
